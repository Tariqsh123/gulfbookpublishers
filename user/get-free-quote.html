Submission Guide:

We’re open to stories of every genre. Whether this is your first book or you’ve published before, Gulf Book Publishers is ready to review your work.

Our editorial team carefully reviews all manuscripts within 48 hours and updates you with the next steps.

To have your book considered, include:

  1. 1 A short cover letter
  2. 2 Your complete manuscript
  3. 3 Details of any previously published books (if applicable)
  4. 4 Your full name, email, and phone number

Fill out our submission form with these details or email us directly at info@gulfbookpublishers.com. Once received, we’ll connect with you quickly.

The Association of Gulf Publishers (AGP) is the national voice of English-language Emirati-owned book publishers. We publish your book on 18+ platforms at affordable prices. From writing to printing, we do it all faster than ever!
